Rubic

Logistics

  • Each Speech must be scored, the score of a speaker will be the average of all his or her speech scores.
  • Make sure you have entered a Username before submission.
  • Do not submit more than one ballot for a particular round under the same username, if you entered the wrong ballot, tell me ASAP.

Score Range

  • Each score should be an Integer between 0-80
  • Average should be around 60
  • Do not give full scores or scores below 40 unless absolutely necessary

Score Breakdown:

Criteria Scores
Style 20
Content 30
Logic 20
Language 10

Explanation:

  • Style: Overall delivery of speech, such as signposting, pause, etc
  • Content: Understanding of the topic demonstrated by speaker in his or her speech. Speaker should be awards a high content score if he or she employed many valid examples to support his or her points.
  • Logic: Logical consistency and validity of the arguments given
  • Language: The quality of sentence structure. Spakers should be given full language scores unless there are sentences which are extremely difficult to understand, or constant use of fillers words such as 'ah-', 'eh-'.
